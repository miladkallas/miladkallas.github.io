Tallinn, Estonia’s capital An amazing place to visit due to its long history and people hospitality. I enjoyed the cold weather and beer and loved its traditional food.

Beirut, Lebanon's capital a small city to visit but full of culture and history. Lebanese were so hospitable, and life lovers. I enjoyed their parties and traditional middle-eastern food.

My love Seville. Seville is the capital of southern Spain’s "Andalusia". I have watched flamenco dancing since my childhood but seeing it in real life is something magical I won't be able to explain. Seville has incredulity warm weather, culture, and people.

Tbilisi is the capital of Georgia, an amazing place to get drunk and full eating delicious food. The old town of Tbilisi a place to spend sleepless hours with wonderful people.

Warsaw, the capital of Poland. I have visited it three times already and each time I fell in love with it, even more, something about the mix of the old history with the temporary architecture of it makes you feel that you are in place that is incredibly special.
